Key Insights

UK-EU Trade

Exports declined slightly by 6%-10%.

Imports remained relatively stable, resulting in a widened trade deficit.

UK-Non-EU Trade

Imports increased notably by over 15%.

Trade deficit narrowed, although overall gains remain limited.

Post-Brexit, the UK's trade landscape has significantly evolved. While EU trade experienced a downturn, non-EU trade relationships have strengthened, particularly with Asia-Pacific and North American regions. This shift reflects the UK's strategic adaptation in establishing new international trade networks.

Russia-Ukraine Conflict
