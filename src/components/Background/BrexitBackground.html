Brexit Background

June 23, 2016

The UK held a referendum on Brexit, with 51.9% of voters supporting leaving the EU.

March 29, 2017

The UK formally triggered Article 50 of the Lisbon Treaty, initiating the Brexit process.

January 31, 2020

The UK officially left the EU, entering a transition period.

December 31, 2020

The transition period ended, and the UK fully exited the EU single market and customs union.

Major Impacts

  • Trade Relations: Need to renegotiate trade agreements with the EU
  • Tariffs and Border Controls: Increased trade barriers and inspection procedures
  • Supply Chain Adjustments: Businesses need to adapt to new trade rules
  • Market Access Changes: UK businesses face new challenges in accessing EU markets
